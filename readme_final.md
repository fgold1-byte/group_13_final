### Game Description
Suitjack is a blackjack inspired game with a twist: the suit of the first card you draw can change the outcome of the round. 
The player begins each round

Garvie, Duncan. “Blackjack - Card Game Rules.” Www.pagat.com, www.pagat.com/banking/blackjack.html.. 
Garvie writes about the game of Blackjack, including how its traditionally a North American casino game. He goes over it overall premise and the rules of the game. This source provide overall background to help inform logic for code from the rules of the game. As well as making clear how the game works.

Pagat.com. (n.d.). Blackjack variants and side bets. https://www.pagat.com/banking/blackjack.html
We looked at this site to research how traditional card games are modified with extra rules. This inspired our "Suit Power" system. By looking at how insurance and side bets work in real casinos, we made the logic for heats insurance and diamond multiplier mechanics to make sure they felt balanced and fair for a single-player game.
