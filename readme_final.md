### Game Description
Suitjack is a blackjack inspired game with a twist: the suit of the first card you draw can change the outcome of the round. 
The game begins when a player enters their name, they are then given 100 coins to play with.
Before each round, the player must decide how many coins they want to gamble, the minimum is one and the maximum is 100.
Two cards are dealt to both the player and dealer.
